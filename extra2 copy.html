Effectiveness of proposed feature-aware few-shot self-supervised learning framework

Wav-CHIME3

Results Analysis in CHiME-3
1) Compared to the input in CHIME-3: Compared with the input noisy speech, the PESQ of MatricGAN-U, MatricGAN-U-SV, STGCSEN, and STGCN-SV increased by 6.97%, 33.81%, and 62.43, 73.4% respectively.
2) In CHiIME-3, the PESQ of MetricGAN-U-SV is improved by 25% compared with MetricGAN-U, and the STGCN-SV is improved by 7% compared with STGCN.

Wav-cocktail party

Results Analysis in cocktail party
1) Compared to the input in cocktail party scene: Compared with the input noisy speech, the PESQ of MatricGAN-U, MatricGAN-U-SV, STGCSEN, and STGCN-SV increased by 14%, 23%, and 36%, 39% respectively.
2) In the cocktail party scene, the PESQ of MetricGAN-U-SV is improved by 8% compared with MetricGAN-U, and the STGCN-SV is improved by 2% compared with STGCN.

Conclusion:
Proposed learning framework does not only been proved to be effective with STGCN (7% improvement in CHIME3 and 3% improvement in cocktail party), but also show good performce with the other speech enhancement models (eg. MetricGAN, 25% improvement in CHiME-3, 9% improvement in cocktail party).
